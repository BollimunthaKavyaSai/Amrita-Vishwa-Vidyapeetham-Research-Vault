FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Instant answers, helpful tips, and community wisdom.

General

  • How do I register?
    As Player or Coach, fill the login form. Account is created instantly.
  • Can I reset my password?
    Yes, click 'Forgot Password' and follow instructions.
  • How do I leave feedback?
    Use the contact form and suggestions section.

Account & Support

  • How do I change my email?
    Go to Profile in Dashboard & update email.
  • Is my data secure?
    Your data is protected and only visible to authorized admins.

Community

  • How to comment on blogs?
    Open any blog post and use the comment box.
  • Can questions be upvoted?
    Yes, click the like button near an answer or comment.
